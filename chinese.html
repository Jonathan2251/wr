Experience¶

My son cannot speak Chinese fluently at age 9 (4th grade). His Chinese grows up very quickly during that year and speaks fluently at age 10. My son cannot speak Taiwanese well although he says he understand my Taiwanese. So it needs 10 years to become a fluent Chinese speaker, I think. Before age 10 he always like to join the conversation between I and my wife, but he cannot speak long sentence fluently. My colleague said: his children are same.
